Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Petcarus ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.

Pet Photos: You may upload photos of your pets. These photos are processed by AI services to generate illustrations and are stored in your account.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including coin balances, generation history, and in-app purchase records.

Device Information: We may collect device identifiers and basic device information for fraud prevention and service stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our pet illustration generation service
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription or coin balance
  • To process in-app purchases through Apple's payment system
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To respond to your feedback or support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Petcarus:

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, and serverless functions
  • Google Gemini — AI-powered pet illustration generation
  • NVIDIA — Pet photo validation and analysis
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management
  • Resend — Delivering feedback emails

Each service processes your data according to their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to perform its function.

4. AI-Generated Content

Petcarus uses artificial intelligence to generate pet illustrations based on photos you provide. AI-generated content is created on-demand and is not used to train AI models. Your original pet photos are not shared with third parties for purposes beyond generating your requested illustrations.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely with Supabase and protected using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and pet photos for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Petcarus is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Delete your account at any time through the app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
